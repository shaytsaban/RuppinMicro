About the Course

This course introduces the fundamental principles of microeconomics, focusing on how individuals and firms make decisions and interact in markets.

  • Understanding supply, demand, and market equilibrium
  • Analyzing consumer behavior and utility maximization
  • Exploring production costs and firm behavior
  • Examining different market structures (e.g., perfect competition, monopoly)
  • Applying concepts of elasticity to real-world scenarios
